Our Story

ZIYA Designs was born from a deep appreciation for Kerala's rich textile heritage. Our founder, inspired by the intricate weaves and vibrant colors of traditional Kerala sarees and fabrics, embarked on a journey to bring this legacy to contemporary fashion.

Each piece in our collection is meticulously crafted by skilled artisans who have inherited centuries-old techniques. We blend these traditional methods with modern design sensibilities to create garments that are both timeless and contemporary.

Our commitment to quality extends beyond aesthetics. We use only the finest fabrics, ensuring that each garment feels as exquisite as it looks. Every piece is custom-stitched to order, allowing for personalized modifications and perfect fit.

Custom Size Measurements

Get a Perfect Fit Every Time

Please provide your measurements in inches for a perfectly tailored fit. Our expert tailors will use these specifications to create a garment that fits you like a dream. Follow the guide below for accurate measurements.

1

Use a Soft Measuring Tape

Always use a flexible cloth measuring tape for accurate measurements. Do not use a metal tape measure.

2

Wear Fitted Clothing

Wear the undergarments you plan to wear with the garment when taking measurements for the most accurate fit.

3

Stand Naturally

Stand straight with your feet together and arms at your sides. Don't suck in your stomach or stand unnaturally.

4

Keep Tape Snug

The tape should be snug but not tight against your body. Make sure it's parallel to the floor for horizontal measurements.
